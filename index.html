kenya

Kenya, country in East Africa famed for its scenic landscapes and vast wildlife preserves. Its
Indian Ocean coast provided historically important ports by which goods from Arabian and Asian traders have entered the
continent for many centuries.

Nairobi
Nairobi was founded in 1899 as a railway depot. It was initially a swampy area. Nairobi National Park is located within the city's limits. The Great Migration passes through Nairobi.
2 / 3
Amboseli National Park
“Home of the African Elephant” Crowned by Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa's highest peak, the Amboseli National Parks is one of Kenya's most popular parks. The name "Amboseli" comes from a Maasai word meaning "salty dust", and it is one of the best places in Africa to view large herds of elephants up close.
3 / 3
mombasa
Mombasa was the capital of Kenya till 1906. Today Nairobi is the capital of Kenya but Mombasa was once the most powerful city in the country. From 1887 to 1906, Mombasa became the capital of Kenya's Protectorate. The capital was later moved because of medical reasons.
